About Us

Welcome to UBTECH INCUBATION, a pioneering force in academic and research services, dedicated to bridging the gap between academia and industry. Founded by Tejas Shingvi and Manisha Shingvi, our journey began in 2017 and we officially registered in 2018. With a steadfast commitment to quality and innovation, we have established ourselves as leaders in providing exceptional support across various fields including Computer Science, IT, Mechanical, Civil, Automation, ENTC, and Electrical engineering.

At UBTECH INCUBATION, our mission is to foster a collaborative and innovative environment that nurtures intellectual growth and professional development. Our team is comprised of experienced professionals, academicians, and industry experts, all dedicated to delivering personalized and high-quality services. We offer comprehensive assistance for PhD candidates, ensuring they receive the guidance and resources needed to excel in their research endeavors.

Whether you are a student seeking research support or a business looking for advanced software solutions, UBTECH INCUBATION is your trusted partner in achieving excellence. Join us as we continue to push the boundaries of knowledge and technology, creating a brighter future for all.
